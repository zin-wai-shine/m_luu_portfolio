INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADDING YOUR BACKGROUND IMAGE
===============================================

1. Place your background image in this folder (src/assets/images/)

2. Name the image file exactly: hero-cinematic-bg.jpg
   (You can also use .png format - just update the import in Home.jsx)

3. The image will automatically be used as the background for the home page hero section

4. Recommended image specifications:
   - Size: 1920x1080 pixels or larger
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - The image should show a cinematic production scene with a camera operator
   - Person with camera should be positioned on the right side of the image

5. After adding the image, the home page will automatically use it as the background.
